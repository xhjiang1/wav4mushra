Evaluation of Synthesized Speech (Quality)

The purpose of this study is to evaluate the perceptual quality and naturalness of synthesized speech. In each test screen, you will see one open reference and eight test items. The reference is provided only for comparison and should not be scored. Please listen carefully and rate the quality of every test item on a scale from 0 to 100, where 0 means extremely poor and 100 means identical in quality to the reference.
Please judge only the audio quality / naturalness of the speech. Do not judge the grammar or the sentence content itself. Some test items may sound very similar to the reference, while some may be clearly degraded. Multiple items are allowed to receive the same score, including 100 if you believe they are as good as the reference.
The overall test will take about 40 minutes. Please rate every item carefully.
  • Please use headphones.
  • Please use a modern browser such as Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • Do not skip any test item. Every slider should be assigned a score.
  • The scale is continuous from 0 to 100. Any integer value can be used.
  • If you have already completed this task very recently, please do not take it again.
